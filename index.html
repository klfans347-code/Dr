The short version

"Every symptom is a clue — my job is reading it right, the first time."

Dr. Arijit Bag is a Consultant Neurologist at Apollo Hospitals, Kolkata, seeing adult patients across the full range of neurological conditions — from recurring headaches to complex seizure disorders.

His approach stays close to the person in front of him: a full history, an unhurried exam, and a plan explained in language that makes sense outside a hospital — not just written on a chart.

New patients are welcome. The fastest way to reach the clinic is directly over WhatsApp.
